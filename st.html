Market Analysis and Country Entry Strategy

The Reason

A large Decorative Paints company in China was evaluating the potential to enter the Vietnamese market.

The Approach

A detailed market analysis of the Vietnamese market using both primary and secondary research modes to ensure accuracy and verified data. A comprehensive study of market potential, size, competitors, customers, costs, localization challenges, regulations and existing sales channels. A detailed strategy plan was drawn out based on the insights and a thorough gap analysis was conducted.

The Result

The outcome of the research analysis and detailed strategy plan resulted in the company deciding to postpone plans to enter Vietnam based on the lack of capabilities required to pursue a successful market expansion plan
